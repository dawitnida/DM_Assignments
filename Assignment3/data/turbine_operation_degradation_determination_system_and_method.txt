TURBINE OPERATION DEGRADATION DETERMINATION SYSTEM AND METHOD . A system for creating a financial risk model for at least one turbine includes a database storing operational characteristics of one or more turbines and a processing module coupled to the database that receives information from the database and creates the financial risk determination. The processing module includes a performance degradation modeler that receives performance data and creates a model there from and a financial risk calculator coupled to the performance modeler that determines financial exposure to a contract based on the model received from the performance modeler.
