Wireless Trading System And Method Of Using The Same . A wireless trading system used for trading by an electronic money card includes a reading device including a reading unit for wirelessly reading account information of the electronic money card and a transmitting unit for transmitting out the account information read by the reading unit, a communication device, a commerce platform for providing the service of trading, and a payment platform for providing the service of paying trading bills. A method of using the above-mentioned wireless trading system includes: the reading device wirelessly reading the account information of the electronic money card and then sending the account information to the communication device, the communication device logging in the commerce platform, some consumption activities being carried out on the commerce platform and bills made by the consumption activities being sent to the payment platform, the payment platform paying for the bills and sending account balance to the communication device, and the communication device modifying the account information of the electronic money card.
