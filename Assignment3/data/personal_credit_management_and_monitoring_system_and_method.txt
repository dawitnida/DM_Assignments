Personal credit management and monitoring system and method . This document discloses fully-integrated consumer credit management and identity theft detection, protection and resolution systems, methods, and computer program products. These solutions enable consumers to manage the entire lifecycle of their credit profile and personal identity through a simple integrated application that combines credit management, credit monitoring, identity theft detection, and identity restoration. The credit management and identity theft protection system integrates the complexities of credit reports and identity theft protection into a single solution giving consumers more control over their financial future than conventional systems.
