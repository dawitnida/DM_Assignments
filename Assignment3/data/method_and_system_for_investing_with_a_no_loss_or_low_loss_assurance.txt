Method and system for investing with a no-loss or low-loss assurance . A method for investing with a no or low-loss assurance, provides a plurality of risk levels. A feasibility analysis is performed to determine a positive percent of profit. The agent purchases an equity with a money packet of the investor based on whether the equity can conform to a gain sale threshold which achieves the goal of the feasibility analysis. When one of the sale thresholds is reached, the equity is sold. If a gain sale threshold is reached, the gain is apportioned between the investor and the agent according to the selected risk level. The method repeats the steps of purchasing an equity, selling the equity when a sale threshold is reached and apportioning the gain between the investor and the agent, until the sale threshold is a loss sale threshold. The loss is apportioned between the investor and the agent based on the selected risk level.
