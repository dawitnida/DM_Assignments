User customizable statistical information system . The present invention comprises a system for surveying and reporting of wage and benefit information, as well as other competitive economic data, to subscriber companies in a manner that permits flexibility in the statistical analysis performed on the data as well as the manner of reporting. A user is able to select which competitors will be used for comparison based on the date on which the survey data was entered for each competitor, and based on the facilities and personnel that a particular competitor may have in common with the user. Where the data entered is job salary and benefit data, users also have the flexibility to request reports by cross-industry occupation so that the data is not limited solely to the particular industry in question even if that industry competes with others for the same types of employees.
