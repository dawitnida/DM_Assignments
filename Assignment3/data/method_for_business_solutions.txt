Method for business solutions . A method for providing business solutions for use by a company that improves the company's performance and profitability incorporates a number of interrelated ideas that can increase the productivity of the company and also lower the cost of products and services provided by that company or by its related companies. The method also provides the necessary capital to expand such a business by manufacturing products in other parts of the world, increases exports for that business and optimizes taxes and merger and acquisition costs.
