Account administration plans and systems . A computer system according to particular embodiments of the invention is adapted for: (A) receiving one or more claims for reimbursement from an account holder of a health savings account; (B) after receiving the one or more claims, allowing a plan service provider to: (1) review each particular one of the one or more claims for reimbursement to determine whether the particular claim is properly reimbursable from the health savings account; and (2) in response to determining that the particular claim is reimbursable from the health savings account, certify that the particular claim is properly reimbursable from the health savings account.
