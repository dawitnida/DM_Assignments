SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR PROVIDING COMPENSATION TO LOAN PROFESSIONALS . A system and method for providing a compensation program associated with a modifiable mortgage is disclosed which provides incentives to encourage a sales force to originate a modifiable mortgage. In one aspect of the invention, the compensation program calculates an annuity to be paid to an appropriate sales force member based on the principal, interest, and/or service income of a modifiable mortgage. In another aspect of the invention, the compensation program provides a commission calculation to be paid based on a triggering event such as, for example, each time the interest rate on a modifiable mortgage is lowered. The compensation program of the present invention may also include both the annuity compensation and the commission compensation as incentives to originate the modifiable mortgage.
