Web site for retail marketing program . A method of marketing comprises determining a cost of at least one item ordered by a user, calculating an amount of credit for use by the user to defray costs of its marketing activities, the amount of credit being based on a cost of the ordered item, providing data relating to the amount of credit to a computer system, and generating a web page using the data provided to the computer system. The web page displays the amount of credit available to the user for its marketing activities. Bonus credit may be earned by the user to defray costs of its marketing activities by advertising a certain item(s) during a predetermined time period or advertising an item for at least a predetermined price.
