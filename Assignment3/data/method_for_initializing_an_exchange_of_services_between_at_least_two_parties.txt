Method For Initializing An Exchange Of Services Between At Least Two Parties . A method for initializing an exchange of services between at least two parties, including transactions in relation to the acquisition of products or the utilization of services. Optical or acoustic information on at least one of the products or services of interest to the user of a mobile terminal are acquired by means of a mobile terminal that is operated in a telecommunication network. The acquired information is transmitted to a data processing system linked with the telecommunication network via a message service of the telecommunication network. The processing system determines additional information on the product or service of interest to the user of a mobile terminal using the acquired information. The additional information so determined is at least partially transmitted to the mobile terminal of the user via a message service of the telecommunication network and is displayed by the mobile terminal.
