METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR DETERMINING CAPITAL INVESTMENT, EMPLOYMENT CREATION AND GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION OF GREENFIELD INVESTMENT PROJECTS . A Project Size Estimation and Triple Weighted Location Assessment Model to estimate the capital investment, employment creation and to determine the highest quality geographic location for a Greenfield investment project, based on algorithms that firstly calculate and apply capital and employment intensity and average project size ratios to estimate capital investment and employment creation for the project and secondly apply a triple weighted quality assessment model to calculate the quality competitiveness of locations for the investment project.
