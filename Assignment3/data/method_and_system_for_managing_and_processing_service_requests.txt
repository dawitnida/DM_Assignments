Method and system for managing and processing service requests . In accordance with various embodiments of the invention, a system and various methodologies are provided that enable proactive analysis of details associated with clients' financial services transactions by the organization(s) providing those services. Additionally, the system and various methodologies generate automated electronic messages that are transmitted to clients to inform them of potential issues effecting their financial service transactions. These automated electronic messages may be reviewed by clients in a secure environment and responded to by the clients with the goal of resolution of identified potential issues.
