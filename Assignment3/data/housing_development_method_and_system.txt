HOUSING DEVELOPMENT METHOD AND SYSTEM . "A system and method for developing and building homes combining energy efficient and environmentally friendly or ""green"" housing technologies with innovative financial programs for the builder and buyer. The system includes a home building company constructing a plurality of homes located within the home development. Each home is developed and operated by utilizing energy efficient and ""green"" housing technologies. A financing scheme for financing the construction of the plurality of homes by the home building company and the purchase of each home by the purchaser is also provided. The financing scheme includes a Public Improvement District (PID) assessment program to finance the home development."
