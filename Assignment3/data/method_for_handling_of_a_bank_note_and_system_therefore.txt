METHOD FOR HANDLING OF A BANK NOTE AND SYSTEM THEREFORE . The present invention relates to a method for handling of a plurality of bank notes comprising the following steps: a) collecting said plurality of bank notes (1) at a location of commerce (2), b) safe-keeping said bank notes (1), wherein the method also comprises c) registering each one of said bank notes (1) by use of an identity code (3) of each bank note (1), in connection with, or subsequent to, collecting said bank notes (1), d) registering and designating said banknotes (1) as being invalid, to minimise the risk of theft of said bank notes (1). The invention also comprises a system for handling of a plurality of bank notes according to the method of the invention.
