SYSTEM FOR ADMINISTERING A GUARANTEED BENEFIT ACCOUNT . A system for administering a guaranteed benefit account held by an individual, including a computer having a central processing unit for processing data, storage means for storing input data related to compensation levels of an individual account holder, contribution patterns of the individual account holder, and time periods from opening of the account until disbursement of account funds for the individual account holder, and, a first arithmetic logic circuit configured to calculate plan disbursements as a guaranteed percentage of individual account holder compensation levels.
