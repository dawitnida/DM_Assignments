Graduation function for stored value card . A stored value card and system provides a stored value card issued in the name of a cardholder and with an account associated with an issuer and a legal account holder. The legal account holder is initially a person or entity other than the cardholder. The legal account holder manages the account and is authorized to add funds to the account and the cardholder is authorized to use the funds in the account. As the account is being used, and account data is changing, the account data is compared to a plurality of triggering criteria. When a predetermined number of the triggering criteria are satisfied, the nature of the account is changed and the cardholder becomes the legal account holder who then manages and owns the funds in the account.
