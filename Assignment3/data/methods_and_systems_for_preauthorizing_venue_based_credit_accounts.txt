Methods and Systems for Preauthorizing Venue-Based Credit Accounts . A method and system for third party preauthorization of spending at a venue. The third party activates, on behalf of a user, a venue-based credit account for a spending transaction. The transaction is enabled only after at a user and/or a user-designee authorize the venue to open the venue-based credit account. The third party activates the venue-based credit account by providing a credit status of the user to the venue on behalf of the user and deactivates the venue-based credit account, by financially settling the transaction on behalf of the user, only after a triggering event.
