METHOD TO COMPETE FOR CREDIT CARD BUSINESS AT THE POINT OF SALE . A method (400) of conducting a financial transaction. The method can include receiving from each of a plurality of credit providers an offer (130) indicating an incentive (125) available to a user for using an established credit account to make payment for the financial transaction at a point-of-sale terminal (110), and presenting the offers to the user. The user can be prompted to select one of the established credit accounts to make payment for the financial transaction.
