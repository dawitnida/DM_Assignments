Method for funding establishment and operation of a trauma center . A method for securing funding for at least one of the establishment and operation of a Level I or Level II trauma center. A community in which a trauma center is to be established and/or maintained is identified. In addition, a plurality of local businesses in the community that may be capable of paying for advertising services within the trauma center are identified. Funds are solicited from the plurality of local businesses, in exchange for at least one specified advertising opportunity within the confines of the trauma center. In embodiment, the local businesses pay a periodic fee for the periodic maintenance of an advertisement within the trauma center.
