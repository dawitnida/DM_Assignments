System for providing user-selected information through internet . A user-selected information providing system has an additional service providing server for providing an additional service about a specific information, a user-selected information providing server linked to the additional service providing server through Internet, for providing by synthesizing a basic information related to a user information, a user-selected information and the additional service provided by the additional service providing server, and a terminal apparatus receiving the basic information, the user-selected information and the additional service by connecting with the user-selected information providing server through the Internet. Further the user-selected information providing server gives a premium depending on the number of the terminal apparatus purchased by users to the terminal apparatus manufacturing company, and receives a premium depending on the number of the users receiving the additional information from the user-selected information providing server.
