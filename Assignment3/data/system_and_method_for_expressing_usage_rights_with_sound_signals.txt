System and method for expressing usage rights with sound signals . A system and method for creating a rights expression for association with an item for use in a system for controlling use of the item in accordance with the rights expression. Rights expression information indicating a manner of use of an item is expressed. A sound signal based representation of the rights expression information is then generated. The sound signal based representation is configured to be interpreted by an interpreter of a system for controlling use of the item to permit enforcement of the rights expression.
