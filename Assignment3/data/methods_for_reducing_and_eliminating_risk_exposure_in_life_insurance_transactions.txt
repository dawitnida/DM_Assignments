Methods for reducing and eliminating risk exposure in life insurance transactions . A method for arranging a life insurance transaction while managing risk exposure associated with the transaction is disclosed. In one embodiment, the method includes coordinating the initiation of an insurance policy that relates to the life of a person. Execution of a loan on behalf of an owner of the policy for the payment of premiums due on the policy is also coordinated with a lending entity. The posting of collateral for the loan by a third party is coordinated, thereby removing risk exposure from the lending entity, the policy owner, and/or the insured.
