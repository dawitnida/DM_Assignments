Method and apparatus to control playback in a download-and-view video on demand system . Rules concerning whether or not to insert advertisement and/or allow trick mode capabilities during playback of locally stored content downloaded from a content provider's or a VOD service providers server are communicated to the client device and may be specified by a variety of mechanisms including a Web-page based tool for a content provider to edit and for a VOD service provider to review and modify such rules. The client device stores and applies the rules during playback of the content.
