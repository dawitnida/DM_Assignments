Alaskan auto trust . The purpose of this invention is to limit the liability of an automobile owner, and to provide a method for securely storing title to automobiles. In order to limit the liability of the automobile owner, the vehicle is placed in a trust. In addition, by placing the vehicle in a trust, the owner is allowed to choose the taxing jurisdiction of the vehicle. The grantor of the Auto Trust can reduce the risk of losing title to the vehicle by housing all titles held in a trust at a centralized, secure, and remote location.
