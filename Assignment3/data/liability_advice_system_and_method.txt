LIABILITY ADVICE SYSTEM AND METHOD . A computerized method and apparatus for providing a borrower with a choice of products relating to new debts or changes to a portfolio of debts. The method can include collecting borrower information using a user interface, building scenarios of debt products currently available to the borrower, evaluating the scenarios of debt products using (i) payment models for the scenarios determined based on interest and loan information and (ii) the borrower information, and selecting one or more of the scenarios based on the evaluation of the scenarios.
