METHODS AND APPARATUS FOR FUNDS REMITTANCES TO NON-PAYMENT CARD ACCOUNTS USING PAYMENT CARD SYSTEM . A method includes receiving a funds transfer request. The request specifies a transaction amount, a destination bank account and a source payment card account. A response to the request includes initiating a payment transaction to transfer the transaction amount from the source payment card account to a payment card system settlement account that was established by a payment card system with an agent bank. The response to the request also includes issuing an instruction for the agent bank to transfer the transaction amount from the settlement account to the destination bank account.
