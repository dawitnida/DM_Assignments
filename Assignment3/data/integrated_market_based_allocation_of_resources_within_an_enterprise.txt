Integrated market-based allocation of resources within an enterprise . Allocating acquired resources within a firm is disclosed. The ability to list acquired resources of a firm on an internal marketplace is provided. A purchasing power asset is divided among the prospective users of an acquired resource of the firm. An intra-firm market enabling prospective users of an the acquired resource of the firm to attempt to obtain the acquired resource by offering to provide an offered amount of the purchasing power asset in exchange for receiving the acquired resource is provided.
