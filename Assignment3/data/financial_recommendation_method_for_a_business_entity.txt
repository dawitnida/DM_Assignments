Financial recommendation method for a business entity . A method of providing a financial recommendation for a business entity includes providing a first set of constraints for a first set of financial categories having at least two spending categories. Predicted values for each of a plurality of financial categories that include at least the first set of financial categories are provided for at least a portion of a business cycle. The predicted values provided satisfy the respective first set of constraints. Based on at least one of the predicted values, at least one financial indicator value associated with at least one period of the business cycle is calculated. If the at least one financial indicator value satisfies the at least one desired financial rating for at least the portion of the business cycle, then at least one of the predicted spending values is provided as a first set of recommended future spending values.
