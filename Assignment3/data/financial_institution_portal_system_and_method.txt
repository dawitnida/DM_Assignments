FINANCIAL INSTITUTION PORTAL SYSTEM AND METHOD . Systems and methods utilizing a portal on a single computer platform for improving risk assessment and business reporting are disclosed. The portal integrates at least two financial information resources such as, for example, an electronic credit report (eCR) database, an electronic know your customer (eKYC) database, a client business management information system, and a customer profitability system large area network. The eCR database includes a link to an exposure database, a credit rating database, and/or an intelligence database. The eKYC database includes an anti-money laundering scoring module, and/or a standard KYC form or an enhanced KYC form.
