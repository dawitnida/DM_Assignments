System and method for increasing office space income to reduce particular tenant(s) office space costs and/or increase landlord(s) income . The disclosed method and system provide a way to increase the income generated from office space to reduce office space rental costs and/or to increase the landlord's income, the system and method involving a business arrangement among one or more of the landlord, good credit tenant(s), and a shared office provider or other having shared office experience/expertise.
