SUBSIDIZER FOR NETWORKED LOAN MARKET AND LENDING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM . Systems, methods, and apparatus for receiving a plurality of borrowing orders from a plurality of borrowers; receiving a plurality of lending orders from a plurality of lenders; receiving at least one subsidizer order from a loan subsidizer that includes one or more loan subsidizing terms; and executing a matching algorithm to match at least some of the borrowing orders with at least some of the lending orders, wherein the result of the executed match is affected by the existence of the at least one subsidizer order.
