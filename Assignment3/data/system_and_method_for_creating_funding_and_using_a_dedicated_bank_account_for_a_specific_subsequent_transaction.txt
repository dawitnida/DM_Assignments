System and method for creating, funding and using a dedicated bank account for a specific subsequent transaction . The invention provides a system and method for the creation of a special purpose gift bond account for the purchase of specific subsequent transaction. A card and card carrier are created and a donor purchases the card and card carrier to give to a recipient who completes online HTML forms to create the recipient's own special gift bond account at a participating bank with the account funded from cash paid for the purchase of the card and card carrier. The funded gift bond bank account is then only used to purchase item(s) for a specific subsequent transaction such as for a US Savings Bond from the US Treasury.
