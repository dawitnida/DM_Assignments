System and method for processing information on goods or service . A service information processing system includes a goods information display unit for retrieving explanation and a price of goods and explanation of financial products and transmitting them to a client terminal; a goods determination judgment unit for retrieving a working term of the financial products from a contract information DB by using identification information of the financial products selected by the client, and starting monitoring whether or not the working term of the financial products is reached; and a good determination acceptance unit for retrieving one or a plurality of goods or services purchasable by a post-working amount obtained as a result of investment of a fund corresponding to the purchase amount in the selection financial products when the working term is reached.
