Business evaluation supporting method . A method and an apparatus for easily and efficiently setting an evaluation index suitable for an evaluation purpose when a business or a business portfolio is evaluated. In the method and apparatus, investment distribution is easily and visually confirmed during computation of the evaluation index when an object to be computed is the business portfolio. A table for defining a computation expression model for the evaluation index and a correspondence relationship between the evaluation index and the evaluation purpose is managed by a database to extract and provide an evaluation index model conforming to the evaluation purpose specified by a user. When the business portfolio is evaluated, business investment distributions are represented by a pie chart. The investment distribution information is updated and the evaluation index is computed, by adjusting a distribution surface of the represented pie chart.
