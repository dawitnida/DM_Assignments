Techniques to manage financial performance data exchange with standard taxonomies . Techniques to manage financial performance data are described. An apparatus may include an online analytical processing database with a multidimensional data model, and a business information exchange module communicatively coupled to the online analytical processing database. The business information exchange module may import a taxonomy to the multidimensional data model, create a business model using the multidimensional data model, and export financial data from the business model to an extensible business reporting language instance document. Other embodiments are described and claimed.
