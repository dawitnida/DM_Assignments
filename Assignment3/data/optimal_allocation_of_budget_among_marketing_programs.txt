Optimal allocation of budget among marketing programs . Techniques for optimizing budget allocations among a plurality of marketing campaigns are described. The techniques include providing an expected return on investment (ROI) for potential contacts in target populations for each of a plurality of marketing campaigns and ordering the potential contacts by ROI for the marketing campaigns. The techniques also include determining a threshold ROI satisfying an overall budget, where potential contacts with ROI greater than the threshold level are recommended for actual contact.
