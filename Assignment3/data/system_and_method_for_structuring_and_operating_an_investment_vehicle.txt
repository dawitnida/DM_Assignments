System and method for structuring and operating an investment vehicle . A system and method of structuring and operating an investment vehicle is disclosed which structures the investment vehicle as a common trust fund which operates using the investment, trading, and timing strategies of a hedge fund. The investment vehicle is implemented using a common trust fund structure, which may be offered by a financial institution. The investment vehicle so operated by the financial institution using a hedging strategy to manage the property from the trust to achieve a strong return, even in adverse market conditions.
