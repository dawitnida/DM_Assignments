SYSTEMS AND METHODS FOR A TRANSACTION VETTING SERVICE . An embodiment relates generally to a method of processing a financial transaction. The method includes receiving a requested transaction, where the requested transaction is transferring a sum of money or other assets from an originator to a receiver. The method also includes applying a set of rules to the requested transaction to vet the requested transaction, where the set of rules define the regulatory and legal compliance rules for the governing jurisdictions of the originator and receiver for a legal transaction. The method further includes obtaining a result from the application of the set of rules and approving the requested transaction in response to the result being passing all requirements of the set of rules.
