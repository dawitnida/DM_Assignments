SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR INVESTMENT IN A PORTFOLIO OF ENTERTAIMENT PRODUCTIONS . "The invention relates to a system and method for raising a fund from investors for the purposes of distributing to a portfolio of startup investments, where each startup investment within the portfolio has been separately capitalized. Additionally, the invention relates to a system and method for investing in a milestone based portfolio of startup investments. The investment then must meet certain preset criteria (""milestones"") before additional capital is allocated to the investment."
