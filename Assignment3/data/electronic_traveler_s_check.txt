Electronic traveler's check . The electronic traveler's check system and method includes an electronic traveler' check card that is usable to retrieve money from point of service locations and banks. The system has a database of user information, such as a pin number and the user's passport number, which is used to verify the identity of the user before a transaction can take place. An emergency credit feature is provided to enable transaction completion when a cardholder's account has insufficient cash funds.
