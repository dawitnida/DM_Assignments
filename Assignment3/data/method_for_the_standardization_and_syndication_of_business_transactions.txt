Method for the standardization and syndication of business transactions . A method for standardizing and syndicating international business transactions over a communications network, comprising the steps of receiving a transactional request containing terms of the business transaction from a client and categorizing the transaction into pre-defined categories. The method retrieves the functional templates for the pre-defined categories from a database and generates functional aspects of the business transaction, such as forms, documents and the like, based on the terms of the business transaction and client information to enable the client to execute the business transaction.
