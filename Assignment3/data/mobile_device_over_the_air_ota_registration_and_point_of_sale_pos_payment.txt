MOBILE DEVICE OVER-THE-AIR (OTA) REGISTRATION AND POINT-OF-SALE (POS) PAYMENT . A method for enabling secure registration of a mobile device OTA and for conducting a financial transaction at a point-of-sale (POS) has been described herein. In one embodiment, a method of mobile device registration over-the-air (OTA) comprises enabling a pre-loaded payment application having payment account information; enabling a certificate request; receiving payment credentials; associating the payment credentials with the payment account information; transmitting the payment account information and the certificate request OTA; and receiving a certificate of registration of the mobile device OTA.
