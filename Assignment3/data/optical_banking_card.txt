Optical banking card . Methods are provided for executing a banking transaction for a customer. Account information is read from an optical banking card for a banking account associated with the optical banking card. The account information includes a current balance for the banking account. A request is received from the customer to execute the banking transaction, which effects a change in the balance of the account. A movement of funds is coordinated in accordance with the request to execute the banking transaction. Updated account information is written to the optical banking card. The updated account information includes a new current balance for the banking account that accounts for the movement of funds.
