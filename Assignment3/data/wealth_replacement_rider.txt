Wealth replacement rider . A method for determining a death benefit with respect to a charitable gift annuity contract, the method determining the death benefit funded on an after-tax basis of the charitable gift annuity. The method comprises determining if a wealth replacement rider has been selected; and if the wealth replacement rider has been selected, calculating the amount of the death benefit to be funded on the after-tax basis. The method further comprises calculating the new charitable gift annuity payout.
