PAYMENT SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR WEB-BASED VIDEO EDITING SYSTEM . A payment system for use with a web-based video editing system is provided. The payment system includes: a purchase subsystem configured to: receive a user request for a data-right combination; create a binding indicative of the data-right combination; and provide the binding for purchase by the user; and a preview subsystem configured to provide a preview of data of the data-right combination prior to the user purchasing the binding.
