Finance applying method on electronic commerce system . The exchange of individual progress data for indicating the progress of the electronic-commerce transaction managed by a center site is set as the security target. A seller creates a financing application including a target transaction set as the security and a suggested financing amount of money, then transferring the financing application to the center site. The center site makes an inquiry of a buyer as to whether or not to approve the disclosure of the applied content to the financial institution. If the inquiry result is the disclosure-capable, the center site transfers, to the financial institution, a financing request together with the progress data, and the suggested financing amount of money. The financial institution, based on the requested content of the financing, makes an examination concerning the financing request, thereby notifying the seller of the examination result.
