System and method for assigning, tracking and storing equivalent emission values of purchased items . The invention relates to a system for assigning, tracking and storing equivalent emission values corresponding to purchased items, the system comprising a transaction system with: identifying means for identifying at least a purchaser and/or a vendor, a data processing unit, and a database connected therewith having stored therein at least a first data-file related to a purchaser and a second data-file related to a vendor, said data processing unit connected or connectable with the identifying means, and arranged to register a transaction or transactions pertaining to at least one product between at least one purchaser and at least one vendor. The database contains for each product a code identifying such product and a pre-selected parameter that is related to an emission value assigned to said product. The invention also relates to a method for assigning, tracking and storing equivalent emission values corresponding to purchased items.
