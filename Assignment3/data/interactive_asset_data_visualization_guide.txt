INTERACTIVE ASSET DATA VISUALIZATION GUIDE . A data visualization method for guiding investment based on market data involves graphically displaying market-related information by arranging a plurality of regions, each representing a component of a universe, in one of multiple interrelated areas, and selecting a reference position based upon a user preference for indicating to a user the advantageousness of investing in a particular component of the universe based upon position of the region corresponding to the particular component relative to the reference position.
