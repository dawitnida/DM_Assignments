SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR COORDINATING STUDENT LOANS . A system and method that allows coordination of loans by providing a website where borrowers may enter information about themselves and receive a listing of multiple lending institutions interested in entering into loan agreements with those borrowers. Other applications of the system and method include, but are not limited to, tracking referrals of borrowers to lending institutions made based on selection criteria submitted by the lending institutions and personal information submitted by the borrower for purposes of collecting a referral fee from the lending institution paid to referring agents.
