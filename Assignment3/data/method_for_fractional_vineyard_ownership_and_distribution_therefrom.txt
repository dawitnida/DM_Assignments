Method For Fractional Vineyard Ownership and Distribution Therefrom . A method of fractional vineyard ownership and distribution of the wine produced therefrom where individuals purchase an interest in a business entity, receiving a fractional ownership of a winemaking enterprise. Thusly, aggregating their capital and allowing for deployment of same to different licensed business entities at the grape growing, fermentation, bottling and labeling, wholesale, and retail phases of the wine production process.
