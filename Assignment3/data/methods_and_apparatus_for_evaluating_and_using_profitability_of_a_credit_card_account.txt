Methods and apparatus for evaluating and using profitability of a credit card account . A system, method, apparatus, means, and computer program code for evaluating one or more credit card accounts and, more particularly, for determining the profitability of one or more credit card accounts. According to embodiments of the present invention, data associated with one or more variables is determined for one or more credit card accounts. Once the data is collected or otherwise determined for a credit card account, it can be used to compute profitability of the credit card account. An account's profitability may then be used to determine a course of action regarding the credit card account.
