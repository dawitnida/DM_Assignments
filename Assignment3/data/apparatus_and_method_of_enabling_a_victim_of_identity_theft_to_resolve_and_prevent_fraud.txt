Apparatus and method of enabling a victim of identity theft to resolve and prevent fraud . A web-based application method that allows victims of identity theft to rectify and prevent further fraudulent activity is comprised of communicating with a central system and receiving a series of actions and prompts by the system. The invention also allows the user to obtain general information relating to the various types of fraud and gives the user the ability to store and be able to archive all information relating to the fraud in one convenient place. Once the user has been guided through all of these actions, all necessary action will have been taken to rectify the present fraud, and also to prevent further fraudulent activity from occurring.
