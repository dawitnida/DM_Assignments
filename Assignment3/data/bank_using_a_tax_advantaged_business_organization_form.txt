Bank using a tax-advantaged business organization form . A method is provided for chartering a family of related companies. The family of related companies includes a bank subsidiary chartered as an industrial loan bank, and chartered and/or operated to provide tax-pass-through treatment for bank earnings to the shareholders of the bank subsidiary. A parent company owns a majority interest, controlling interest in or otherwise controls the bank subsidiary, the parent company's entity form permits corporations to be shareholders. The bank subsidiary and parent company together have or is operated to provide tax-pass-through treatment to the shareholders of the parent company with respect to earnings from the banking operations of the bank subsidiary
