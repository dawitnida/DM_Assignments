Real Estate Price Indexing . Among other things, transactions involving assets that share a common characteristic are represented as respective data points associated with values of the assets, the data points including transaction value information. Parameters that fit probability distribution functions to at least two respective components of a value spectrum of the data points are determined. The probability distribution function for at least one of the components comprises a power law. An index is formed of values associated with the assets using at least one of the determined parameters.
