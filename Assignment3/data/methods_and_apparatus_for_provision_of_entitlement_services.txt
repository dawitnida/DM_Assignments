Methods and apparatus for provision of entitlement services . Disclosed are novel methods and apparatus for provision of efficient, effective, and/or flexible provision of entitlement services. In accordance with an embodiment of the present invention, a method of providing an entitlement is disclosed. The method includes: receiving a request for the entitlement; verifying the entitlement is granted; creating the granted entitlement; storing information corresponding to the requested entitlement; tracking a consumption of the entitlement based on the stored information; and accessing the entitlement so long as at least one use of the entitlement remains based on the stored information; and an expiration date corresponding to the entitlement is unreached.
