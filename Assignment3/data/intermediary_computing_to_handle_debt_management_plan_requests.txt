Intermediary computing to handle debt management plan requests . A method for Internet-based intermediary computing to handle debt management plan requests, the method including the steps of: receiving respective debt management plan requests at an intermediary computer, by Internet communication, from each of a plurality of request-originating computers; processing the respective debt management plan requests to produce respective, formatted and at least partially validated debt management plan requests; using the respective, formatted and at least partially validated debt management plan requests in enabling a respective debt management plan decision at one of the intermediary computer and a creditor computer; and electronically communicating a signal of the decision to the request-originating computer.
