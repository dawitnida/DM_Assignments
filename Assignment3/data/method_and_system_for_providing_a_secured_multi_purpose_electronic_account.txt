Method and system for providing a secured multi-purpose electronic account . An exemplary embodiment of the invention is a method for providing online commerce. The method includes receiving a request for credit functionality from a customer and establishing credit account if the customer meets credit worthiness requirements of a credit provider. An account identifier is associated with the credit account. The method also includes receiving a request for debit functionality that includes debit source information. The debit worthiness of the debit source is verified and the account identifier is associated with the debit source.
