Method and apparatus for capture and application of legal personal net worth information . A program and database with interactive forms operating on a personal computer or workstation to allow a user to capture, store, retrieve, and report on all legally required information defining an individual person's net worth, for official use in civil legal proceedings. The user sees logically-related sequence of requests for input, while navigating conveniently to specific points in the sequence. Non-relevant forms are suppressed. Partial completion of inputs and resumption of input requests is done at the user's convenience. Valid and consistent relationships are established and maintained among data elements stored. The primary output is a statement of net worth which meets all New York State requirements for content and presentation.
