Methods of operating a self-service check depositing terminal to provide a tracking receipt . A method of operating a self-service check depositing terminal is provided. The method comprises receiving a check from a depositor, attempting to return the check to the depositor, printing a tracking receipt when the check is unable to be returned to the depositor, and dispensing the printing tracking receipt to the depositor to provide the depositor with information regarding next steps to take to resolve non-return of the check.
