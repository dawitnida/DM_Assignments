Method for developing and implementing an employee incentive plan . The employee incentive plan provides a method and system for tracking, calculating and implementing a self-adjusting employee incentive plan. The present invention provides a method and system for establishing an incentive plan, aligning the incentive plan to the employer's management and human resources goals, determining key attributes and related ranges, defining success factors, assessing incentive program reward constraints, and recommending a suitable employee incentive plan based upon all of the generated factors and inputs.
