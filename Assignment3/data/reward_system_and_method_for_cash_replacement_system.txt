REWARD SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR CASH REPLACEMENT SYSTEM . A reward system and method for cash replacement cards wherein a card is issue by or other financial institution to a cardholder. Such a card may be a credit or debit card and provides for a reward, not available to the general public, to the cardholder,based on his or her usage,in the form of a commodity that has a known history of increasing in value over time,such as diamonds.
