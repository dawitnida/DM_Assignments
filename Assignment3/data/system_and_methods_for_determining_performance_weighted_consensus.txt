System and methods for determining performance-weighted consensus . Systems that rank investment recommendations according to the relative historical performance of the sources of the recommendations are provided. In certain embodiments, the present invention provides systems that rank investment vehicles according to relative likely future performance, derived from the average relative historical performance of the advisory services or systems that currently recommend those vehicles. The present invention relates primarily to systems using automated computation, i.e., computer systems and software, but is not limited to such systems.
