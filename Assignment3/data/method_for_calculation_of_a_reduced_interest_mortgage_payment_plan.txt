METHOD FOR CALCULATION OF A REDUCED INTEREST MORTGAGE PAYMENT PLAN . "A ""principal priority reduction"" mortgage which provides both a method to reduce interest paid at a given interest rate and to increase a homeowner's equity rapidly in an otherwise conventional loan scenario. Included is a method for calculating and implementing same. Loan payments are applied to principal due first, while interest accrues and is paid only after the full reduction of the principal. The method may provide an investment vehicle which has particular interest to a certain category of montage investor."
