Platform to Perform Remote Commercial Transactions by Means of Vending Machines . A new system for the sale of intangible products through vending machines, featuring a controlling device placed within a vending machine, an application for the member of the staff of the vending machine (VM) operator working in the field (which runs on his/her cell phone), one or more applications for the members of the staff of the VM operator working within the facilities of the VM operator (running on personal computers), and a computer system which manages all of the above-mentioned components.
