System and method for routing Internet transactions from within an organizational network . A method for receiving credit for revenue generating activities conducted on one or more designated Internet domains through an organization's internal network includes the steps of: detecting that a user of the internal network has requested access to one of the designated domains; routing user access to the designated domain; sending a signal indicating at least one of the user's access of and request to access the designated domain; and receiving at least partial credit for the at least one of the user's access and request to access the designated domain. In at least one embodiment, user access is routed to the designated domain through a proxy server.
