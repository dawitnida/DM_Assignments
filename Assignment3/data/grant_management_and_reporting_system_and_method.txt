Grant, management and reporting system and method . A method and system for managing and reporting grants is disclosed and comprises a processor (18) and a data store (19). A regulatory form associated with a grant (22) which is used by a grantor (12) to administer the grant. A grant set-up module (50) is provided for collecting data associated with the grant which is stored in the data store. A grantee data entry module (60) is provided in which the grantee can enter data related to the grant set-up data which is stored in the data store, the grantee data and the grant set-up data can be compared by the processor to enable a grantor to track the status of the grant.
