System and method for payment transfer . A system and method for payment transfer between a paying party and a recipient party. Optionally and preferably, payment transfer is made by using a prepaid card, which has many security and administrative advantages. Other payment methods may also optionally be used. Also, optionally a plurality of micropayments are provided to a plurality of recipients. The paying party is optionally a media content provider while the recipient party is optionally a user who provides content, although many different combinations of paying/recipient parties may optionally be implemented.
