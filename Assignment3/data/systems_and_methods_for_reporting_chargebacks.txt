Systems and Methods for Reporting Chargebacks . Systems and methods for reporting a chargeback are provided. In this regard, a representative method, among others, includes retrieving customer transaction data related to a closed account, the customer transaction data including at least one of the following: transaction amount, transaction date, transaction identification number, credit rating and authorization date; determining whether any inconsistencies exist based on the retrieved customer transaction data; and responsive to determining that the customer transaction has inconsistencies, reporting a chargeback to an acquirer bank.
