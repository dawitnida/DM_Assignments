CONTINUOUS MEASUREMENT AND INDEPENDENT VERIFICATION OF THE QUALITY OF DATA AND PROCESSES USED TO VALUE STRUCTURED DERIVATIVE INFORMATION PRODUCTS . One embodiment of the present invention relates to a system for measurement and verification of data related to at least one financial derivative instrument, wherein the data related to the at least one financial derivative instrument is associated with at least a first financial institution and a second financial institution, and wherein the first financial institution and the second financial institution are different from one another.
