Computer product for outputting evaluation result . A computer-readable recording medium stores a computer program for outputting an evaluation result of a target to be evaluated by positioning the target on a chart that is formed with a plurality of coordinate axes that determine an evaluation value of the target. The computer program makes a computer execute storing setting conditions of a specific area that indicates a specific target to be evaluated on the chart; and outputting the specific area and a position of coordinates of the target on the chart based on the setting conditions stored at the storing.
