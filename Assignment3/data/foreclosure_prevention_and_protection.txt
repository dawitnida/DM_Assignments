FORECLOSURE PREVENTION AND PROTECTION . A method for process monitoring is disclosed. A borrower is offered a foreclosure prevention and protection plan associated with a secured loan. A periodic payment is received from the borrower in response to the borrower accepting the foreclosure prevention and protection plan. The foreclosure prevention and protection plan allows for at least a portion of a loan payment for the secured loan to be covered by a foreclosure prevention and protection (FPP) entity on behalf of the borrower in response to a covered event occurring. The foreclosure prevention and protection plan additionally may allow for paying/cancelling the debt's investor in response to loss of the property by the borrower.
