Method of automating the process of purchasing and managing annuities . A graphical user interface and computer interoperability system for opening and managing annuity contract accounts is taught herein. User entry interview information is requested based on the type of annuity sought. Features include real time error checking and validation, automatic forwarding of completed forms to financial institutions, and automated response and status information from the financial institutions to users. Automated qualification for Registered Investment Advisors is also provided.
