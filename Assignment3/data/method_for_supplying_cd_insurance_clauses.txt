Method for supplying cd insurance clauses . Disclose herein is a method for supplying CD insurance clauses, which provides the CD-stores insurance clause, in which a corresponding insurance clause and an insurance clause previously made are stored in a CD, provided to an insured party at the time of making a contract. The method includes the steps of storing insurance clauses provided by insurance companies on an insurance database of a clause provision web server; storing related insurance company, each of kinds of insurance of each insurance company, each of main insurances of each insurance company and each of composite insurances of each insurance company, and enabling each insurance company to provide the related insurance clauses to an insured party; ad an insured party accessing the clause provision web server and searching the insurance database to read his insurance clauses.
