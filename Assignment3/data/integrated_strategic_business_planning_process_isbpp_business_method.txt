Integrated strategic business planning process (ISBPP) business method . "Various embodiments of the invention include a financial and operations business planning process to provide a balanced and integrated operating plan for an automotive supplier firm. One embodiment includes a ""Free Flow"" Marketing Plan, several functional plans, and a financial plan. The executive management team sanctions and institutionalize the process within its individual business line. Individual functional manager provide the required plan detail to balance and integrate with other functional plans."
