SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR PREDICTING THE FINANCIAL HEALTH OF A BUSINESS ENTITY . A method for predicting the financial health of a business entity is provided. The method comprises generating one or more anomaly scores and one or more multi-dimensional time-varying patterns for one or more financial metrics related to a business entity and analyzing the one or more anomaly scores and the one or more multi-dimensional time-varying patterns for the one or more financial metrics, using a dynamic predictive modeling system. The method then comprises predicting one or more business behavioral patterns related to the business entity based on the step of analyzing and aggregating the one or more predicted business behavioral patterns in a selected manner to predict the financial health of the business entity.
