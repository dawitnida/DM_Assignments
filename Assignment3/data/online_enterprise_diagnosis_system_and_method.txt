Online enterprise diagnosis system and method . This specification discloses a system and method that are used in an environment of network transmissions to set up a professional consulting website. Through real-time interactions with a user via a network, the user can enter financial data of the enterprise for the past few years. An industrial database is further used to compare the competitive power and kernel potential of the company with other companies in the same industry. This disclosed system can provide an enterprise a management diagnosis report in the format of web page tables in real time so that the enterprise owner can immediately learn of the financial diagnosis opinions, while at the same time maintain the privacy of commercial secrets.
