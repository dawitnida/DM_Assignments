System and method for apportioning commitment updates . A method and system for apportioning financial commitment updates of business partners. According to one embodiment, a contract accounting system receives through a user interface criteria for selecting a subset of business partners whose financial commitments are to be updated, calculates an updated financial commitment of each of the subset of business partners during a predetermined period of time, and transfers the updated financial commitment of each of the subset of business partners to a financial system that maintains current liability information associated with each business partner.
