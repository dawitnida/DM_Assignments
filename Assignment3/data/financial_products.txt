Financial Products . A method and apparatus for providing a financial product from a first party to a second party, wherein a processing system (10) is configured to receive loan parameters relating to a loan from the first party to the second party; and, determine, using the loan parameters and a repayment rule: payments to be paid by the second party to the first party, and an allocation defining: a first portion used for at least partially repaying the principal and/or interest on the loan; and, a second portion used for at least partially acquiring an investment for the second party. The method and apparatus also allows for the financial product to be maintained.
