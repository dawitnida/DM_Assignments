System for facilitating turnkey real estate investment in Mexico . The present invention provides a system of establishing a secure marketplace for the development of real estate projects in Mexico at a competitive price. Particularly, this invention involves a system where investment opportunities for Mexican projects are offered to individuals such as an investor with credit or capital residing in the United States. This method involves compiling data that includes the project, investment incentives and financing options for the investor. This system will maximize the ability to reach potential consumers as well as create investment opportunities in Mexico.
