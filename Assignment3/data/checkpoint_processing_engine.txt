Checkpoint processing engine . A method of correlating at least one data element used by at least one step of a business activity with an instance of said business activity, includes the steps of creating a unique identifier which is distinct from the data element, creating a data table of the data element associated to the unique identifier, identifying the data element used within the step, and correlating the step with the desired instance of said business activity utilizing the data element within the step, compiling a summary of the instance of the business activity and utilizing the summary to analyze the instance of the business activity.
