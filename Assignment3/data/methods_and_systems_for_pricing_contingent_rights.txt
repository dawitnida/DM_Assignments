METHODS AND SYSTEMS FOR PRICING CONTINGENT RIGHTS . The present invention relates to providing a contingent right to purchase an event ticket within an integrated rights marketplace, wherein the cost of the contingent right is based at least in part on a measure of demand for the contingent right. In embodiments, the present invention may provide a contingent event certificate market platform, and select a currency for the contingent event certificate market platform based on an aspect of the user of the platform.
